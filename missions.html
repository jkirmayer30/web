Missions

FSX contains a variety of missions, ranging from taking off and landing on a 4 mile test runway in California in the easiest plane, to tackling the harsh climates of the Barents sea with an engine fire.

Here is a list of my top 5 favorite missions with their descriptions

  1. Limited Options - It's the ultimate test of skill as you face unexpected circumstances high above the Indian Ocean in a 737-800.
  2. Yakutat Mail Run - Haul a load of mail over the mountains from Canada to Yakutat, Alaska. Try to get in before a big snow storm arrives.
  3. Telluride Landing - Fly the challenging visual approach to the Telluride Airport in the mountains of Colorado.
  4. Carribean Landing - Fly a visual approach to the tropical island of St. Mart
  5. Sitka Approach - Enjoy a leisurely VFR approach to Sitka, Alaska over some stunning terrain.
