The Default Planes

FSX comes with a wide variety of default planes, 28 in the best editions. You can see them all here.

Here are a few of my personal favorites

Boeing 737-800

Originally built by boeing in the late 1990s with many still on order today the 800 variant of the 737 is a favorite among many players, it is the premier option for anything from a short flight to a transcontinental long haul. Handling is pleasant and easy. While the game limits it's speed at 330 KIAS before the overspeed alarm, a few changes to the config file will let you cruise at normal airline speeds without such difficulties.

Bombardier Canadair CRJ 700

A great regional jet, both fast and efficient. It's a clear first choice for short routes between cities, and a low touchdown speed around 125KIAS makes it ideal for shorter and less maintained runways.

Augusta Westland EH101

Multi purpose helicopter for both civilian and military usage, a favorite for landing at any type of remote site, on top of a building, on a road, and even on a runway.
